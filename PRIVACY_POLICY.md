# Privacy Policy for PinMaster

**Last Updated: January 12, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how PinMaster ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our browser extension.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

**PinMaster is designed with privacy in mind.**

*   **No Personal Data Collection**: We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal browsing history, Pinterest passwords, or personal images to our servers.
*   **Local Processing**: All image processing, downloading, and file organization happens locally on your device properly using your browser's native download manager.

## 2. Permissions

We require specific permissions to function:
*   **Storage**: To save your preferences (e.g., "Full Metadata Mode" settings) and license status locally.
*   **Downloads**: To save images to your computer.
*   **Host Permissions (Pinterest)**: To script the active tab and extract image URLs from boards you visit.

## 3. Third-Party Services

*   **Gumroad (License Verification)**: If you use the PRO version, we send your License Key to Gumroad's API to verify its validity. No other data is shared.
    *   [Gumroad Privacy Policy](https://gumroad.com/privacy)
*   **Tawk.to (Support)**: The extension allows you to open a support chat window. Using this feature connects you to Tawk.to.
    *   [Tawk.to Privacy Policy](https://www.tawk.to/privacy-policy/)
